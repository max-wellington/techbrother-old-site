Why We Started TechBrother

We saw caregivers losing time to slow computers, unreliable WiFi, and confusing support lines. Residents waited while staff tried to reset systems instead of providing care. TechBrother was created in Tampa to change that. Our work keeps technology out of the way so your team can stay with residents.

Based In Tampa, Focused On Senior Living

We live and work in Tampa Bay. Our clients are assisted living and independent living communities across Hillsborough, Pinellas, and Pasco counties. We know the local vendors, the building layouts, and the daily pressures facing your staff.

When front desk systems freeze or WiFi drops in a dining room, we understand the impact on resident experience. We respond quickly, arrive onsite when needed, and communicate clearly with every staff member involved.

Our Approach: Steady, Preventive Care

Reliable technology should feel quiet and dependable. We watch over your networks, shared drives, and Microsoft 365 accounts so issues are resolved before they interrupt medication passes or family visits.

When problems do appear, you reach our local technicians directly. We document every fix and follow up to confirm staff feel comfortable with the solution.

What Makes Us Different

Many providers serve every type of business. We serve only senior living. That focus means we understand:

  • Your Daily Workflow: Medication cart devices, nurse stations, and community inboxes must stay ready every shift.
  • Your Privacy Standards: Resident information needs calm, consistent protection without complicating staff routines.
  • Your Growth Plans: Expanding wings or adding new amenities requires careful network planning and vendor coordination.
  • Your Budget Expectations: Predictable pricing and clear priorities keep leadership informed and confident.
