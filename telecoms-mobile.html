Telecoms & Mobile Solutions for Marketing Agencies

Modern communication solutions for marketing agencies. VoIP phone systems, mobile device management, and dedicated internet connections to keep your team productive and connected, whether in the office or working remotely.

Why Telecoms & Mobile Solutions Matter: Effective communication is essential for marketing agencies to collaborate with clients, coordinate team projects, and maintain productivity. Modern telecoms and mobile solutions provide the flexibility, reliability, and features your agency needs to stay connected and competitive.

VoIP Telephone Systems

Cloud-based phone systems with advanced features like call forwarding, voicemail to email, and video conferencing. Cost-effective and scalable VoIP solutions that grow with your agency and support remote work.

VoIP Features:

  • Cloud-Based Phone System: No on-premise hardware required, easy to scale and manage remotely
  • Advanced Call Features: Call forwarding, voicemail to email, auto-attendant, and call routing
  • Video Conferencing: Integrated video calling for client meetings and team collaboration
  • Mobile Integration: Use your business phone number on smartphones and tablets
  • Cost Savings: Lower costs compared to traditional phone systems with better features

Business Mobile Phones

Mobile device procurement, setup, and management. Ensure your team has the right devices with proper security and management policies, whether company-owned or BYOD.

Mobile Phone Services:

  • Device Procurement: Help select and purchase the right smartphones for your team's needs
  • Initial Setup: Configure phones with business apps, email, and security settings
  • Mobile Plans: Coordinate with carriers to get competitive business mobile plans
  • Device Management: Centralized management of mobile devices and policies
  • Support & Troubleshooting: Ongoing support for mobile device issues and questions

Dedicated Leased Lines

High-speed, reliable internet connections for agencies that need guaranteed bandwidth and uptime. Perfect for cloud-based workflows, large file transfers, and ensuring consistent connectivity for your team.

Leased Line Benefits:

  • Guaranteed Bandwidth: Dedicated bandwidth ensures consistent speeds regardless of network usage
  • High Reliability: Service level agreements ensure high uptime and quick issue resolution
  • Symmetric Speeds: Equal upload and download speeds for efficient cloud workflows
  • Low Latency: Better performance for real-time applications and video conferencing
  • Scalability: Easy to upgrade bandwidth as your agency grows

Mobile Device Management

Secure management of smartphones and tablets used by your team. Enforce security policies, manage apps, and ensure compliance across all devices to protect business data and client information.

Mobile Device Management Features:

  • Device Enrollment: Streamlined setup and enrollment of mobile devices in your management system
  • Security Policies: Enforce password requirements, encryption, and other security measures
  • App Management: Control which apps can be installed and ensure business apps are available
  • Remote Management: Update settings, deploy apps, and manage devices remotely
  • Compliance Enforcement: Ensure devices meet security and compliance requirements

Unified Communications

Integrated communication platforms that combine phone, video, chat, and collaboration tools in one solution. Improve team collaboration and efficiency with unified communications that work seamlessly across devices.

Unified Communications Features:

  • Integrated Platform: Phone, video, chat, and collaboration in one unified system
  • Cross-Platform: Works on desktop, mobile, and tablet devices
  • Presence Management: See when team members are available for calls or chat
  • File Sharing: Share files and documents during conversations
  • Integration: Integrate with Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and other business tools

Communication Support & Maintenance

Ongoing support and maintenance for all communication systems. We ensure your phones, internet, and mobile devices work reliably, with quick resolution of any communication issues.

Support Services:

  • Issue Resolution: Quick response and resolution of phone, internet, and mobile device issues
  • System Updates: Keep communication systems updated with latest features and security patches
  • Performance Monitoring: Monitor system performance and address issues proactively
  • User Support: Help your team with questions about using communication tools
  • Vendor Management: Coordinate with telecom providers and carriers on your behalf
