Privacy Policy

How TechBrother collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

We take privacy seriously. This page explains what we collect when you contact us, how we process that information, and the choices you have regarding your personal data.

1. Who We Are

TechBrother (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides managed IT services and consulting for small and medium-sized businesses as well as marketing agencies. We are based in Tampa, Florida, and can be reached at contact@techbrother.io or (813) 438-1374.

2. Information We Collect

When you complete the contact form on our website or engage with our embedded scheduling widgets, we may collect:

  • Contact details such as your name, company, email address, and phone number
  • Project details or questions you share in the message field
  • Technical metadata from the form provider (for example, submission timestamp or IP address)

Our site may also collect basic analytics data (like page views and referrers) through standard web server logs or analytics tools. This information is aggregated and does not directly identify you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information you submit to:

  • Respond to enquiries and schedule consultations
  • Prepare proposals or service recommendations tailored to your needs
  • Send follow-up communications related to your request
  • Improve our services and understand demand for our offerings

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses form and scheduling widgets provided by LeadConnector (HighLevel), which processes submissions on our behalf. The information you submit through these forms is securely stored in their platform and made available to us for follow-up. LeadConnector is contractually obligated to protect that data and only process it according to our instructions.

We may also use trusted vendors for website hosting, analytics, email, and CRM. Each vendor is selected for its commitment to safeguarding customer data.

5. Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as needed to respond to your enquiry, provide services, or maintain client records. You can request that we delete your information at any time using the contact details provided above.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Limit or object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, email contact@techbrother.io with your request. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we manage. While no online service can guarantee absolute security, we take steps to minimise risk and to work with vendors who maintain strong security practices.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post any updates on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
